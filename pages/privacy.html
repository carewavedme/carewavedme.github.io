Privacy Policy

At CricBrawl, we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide to us when using our website, mobile application, and other services offered by CricBrawl. By using our platform, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Scope of the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected by CricBrawl from users of our website, mobile application, and other services. It does not apply to information collected by CricBrawl through other means.

Information We Collect

CricBrawl may collect both personal and non-personal information from users in various ways, including:

  • Information provided by users during registration or account setup, such as name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and other relevant details.
  • Information collected automatically when users interact with our platform, including IP address, device information, browser type, and usage data.
  • Information obtained from third-party sources or through third-party integrations, where permitted by law.
  • We collect this information for purposes such as providing and improving our services, personalizing user experiences, communicating with users, and ensuring the security of our platform.
Use of Information

CricBrawl uses the information collected for various purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving our services.
  • Personalizing user experiences and content.
  • Communicating with users about their accounts, transactions, and promotions.
  • Sending marketing communications, newsletters, and promotional offers.
  • Analyzing user preferences, trends, and behavior to enhance our services.
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements.
Third-Party Links and Advertising

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or services, and we may use third-party advertising partners to display advertisements. These third-party websites and services have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices. Users should review the privacy policies of these third parties before providing any personal information.

Data Security

CricBrawl employs physical, electronic, and managerial measures to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and security of user information. We use encryption, firewalls, access controls, and other security technologies to safeguard user data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Account Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated personal data from our system. Upon receiving such a request, we will permanently delete your account and all personal information associated with it, except where retention is necessary for compliance with legal obligations, resolving disputes, or enforcing our agreements.

Please note that deleting your account may not necessarily remove all content or information that you have provided to us, as some data may be retained in backups or archives for a certain period of time. However, such data will be securely isolated and will not be accessible except as required by law.

To request the deletion of your account, please fill out information in Request Account Deletion page. We may require verification of your identity before processing your request.

Children's Privacy

Our Platform is not intended for children under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to request deletion of the information.

Access from Outside the US

While our services are primarily intended for users in the United States, personal and non-personal data may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in other countries where our service providers are located. By using our services, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Tax and Regulatory Filing

In certain circumstances, such as when users withdraw funds or have winnings in excess of required thresholds, we may require users to provide additional information, including social security numbers, for identity verification and tax purposes. We handle this information in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

Revisions to the Privacy Policy

CricBrawl reserves the right to update or revise this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of any material changes to this Privacy Policy by posting the updated version on our platform or by other means as required by law. Users are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed about our data practices.

Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact us at info@cricbrawl.com.
