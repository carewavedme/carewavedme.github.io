Responsible Play

At CricBrawl, we are committed to promoting responsible gaming practices and ensuring the well-being of our users. We recognize that while gaming can be enjoyable, it's essential to maintain a healthy balance and exercise self-control. To support responsible play, we have implemented the following measures

Age Verification

We require all users to be at least 18 years old to participate in contests and use our platform. We may request age verification documentation to ensure compliance with this requirement.

Self-Exclusion

We offer self-exclusion options for users who wish to take a break from gaming or restrict their access to our platform temporarily or permanently. Users can request self-exclusion by contacting our support team.

Play Within Your Means: Please play responsibly and only deposit funds that you can afford to spend on gaming activities. It's essential to set realistic budgets and avoid chasing losses.

Seek Help if Needed

If you or someone you know is struggling with gambling addiction or related issues, please reach out to us at info@cricbrawl.com. We are here to assist you and provide support.

Additional Resources

There are various organizations and resources available to individuals who need help with problem gambling. The National Council On Problem Gambling operates a 24-hour confidential toll-free helpline that you can reach by phone or text at 1-800-522-4700 or chat at ncpgambling.org/chat.

By promoting responsible gaming practices and providing support to our users, we aim to create a safe and enjoyable gaming environment for everyone. We encourage users to play responsibly, set limits, and seek help if needed to ensure a positive gaming experience. If you or someone you know is struggling with gaming addiction or related issues, please reach out to our support team or seek assistance from a qualified professional. Your well-being is our top priority at CricBrawl.
