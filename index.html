About Us

CareWave DME is dedicated to offering high-quality medical equipment to support patient health and recovery. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life through reliable and accessible medical products.

Our Vision: To be the leading provider of durable medical equipment, committed to excellence and patient care

Our Mission: To deliver exceptional service and innovative medical equipment solutions that meet the unique needs of our customers

Services

At CareWave DME, we offer a range of durable medical equipment to support various health needs, including:

Blood Glucose Monitors (Mail Order)

Mail order services for blood glucose monitors and supplies offer a convenient way for patients to receive essential monitoring tools directly at their doorstep.

Orthoses (Off-the-Shelf)

Pre-fabricated external devices that provide immediate support for musculoskeletal conditions, offering a cost-effective and accessible alternative to custom orthotics.
